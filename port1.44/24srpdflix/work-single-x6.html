Hacking consumer decision-making

By understanding the consumer's psyche, one can effectively influence their decision-making process in an ethical manner.

In the realm of advertising, comprehending consumer behavior serves as the cornerstone of effective marketing strategies. Marketers have turned to psychology to unravel the mysteries behind why individuals click, and rightfully so, as psychology underpins every action we take.

Successful campaigns skillfully employ emotional triggers, whether through humor, relatable content, or heartwarming stories. By tapping into these emotional avenues, advertisers can establish a deeper connection with their audience, fostering engagement and driving desired outcomes.

When approaching digital ads that perform I employ 3 easy methods for triggering an emotional connection instead of a logical one.

3 easy methods for making an emotional connection:

• Social Proof

• FOMO!

• Cognitive Biases
