Chick Chips

Delicious and nutritious chickpea-based chips for individuals who value the benefits of plant-based eating.

Project Details


Move Over Pringles, There's a New Chip in Town

Chick Chips provide a delicious and nutritious snacking option for health-conscious individuals who value the benefits of plant-based eating. These chickpea chips are made with the highest quality ingredients, using only whole, organic, non-GMO chickpeas and other natural seasonings, to create a guilt-free snack that satisfies cravings without sacrificing health. Packed with protien to build muscle and stamina. Chick Chips promote wellness and inspire mindful snacking habits that contribute to a happier, healthier lifestyle.

Role: Creative Direction, Visual Identity, CPG Design, Marketing Strategy & Design

Time Frame: 2 Week Project, 2023

Packaging Tube Design

Move Over Pringles

In a head-to-head taste test, Chick Chips outperformed potato chips in terms of flavor and satisfaction.

Protein Packed

Chips with an impressive 20 grams of protein per serving, along with various other essential nutrients, allow customers to enjoy guilt-free snacking without feeling like they're cheating.
