Hi I'm Maggie!

My design journey began in the world of print and branding at a creative agency, where I mastered the art of visual storytelling. Transitioning into marketing for a major home builder allowed me to harness the potential of cohesive campaigns. My path then led me to the entertainment industry, where I contributed to comprehensive worldwide campaigns for major film and television releases during my tenures at FX and later at Sony Pictures.

Most recently, I have honed my skills as a lead growth designer for Techstyle Fashion Group's wide range of subscription products, with a primary focus on creating visually engaging and user-friendly solutions that drive conversions. Furthermore, I am currently working as a freelance designer, enthusiastically taking on new and stimulating projects to continuously broaden my creative horizons. My comprehensive approach to design is influenced by these varied experiences, enabling me to create visually compelling, user-centric, and impactful solutions.

Explore my portfolio to witness how my journey has shaped my design philosophy and to uncover the privilege of collaborating with exceptional companies and clients.
