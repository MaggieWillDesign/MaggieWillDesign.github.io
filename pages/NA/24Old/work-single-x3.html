First Trade

First Trade is an online trading platform pivoting to excite a new generation of investors by providing an innovative and intuitive product.

Project Summary


The Problem

First Trade is in need of a new identity, voice and tone to reposition itself in the market. With many younger generations being drawn to crypto platforms due to the innovation and fresh brand identity many traditional platforms are losing market share.

The Solution

First Trade seeks to address this by refreshing its look and providing a platform to intrigue new investors with ai solutions and educational resources.

Role: Research, Brand and UX Audit, Brand Identity, Brand Voice & Tone, UX, Prototyping, UI, Visual Design, Growth Marketing Strategy & Design

Time Frame: 3 Week Project, 2023

Value Proposition

The First Trade platform is a gateway to the revitalized investment experience. The new and improved dashboard offers cutting-edge AI solutions, and educational resources to empower tomorrow's investors today.

Brand Book Preview

Personalization

The integration of AI has made First Trade more informative and personalized. Users report feeling more engaged and informed about their investments.

Predictive Analytics

The platform employs AI-driven predictive analytics to provide users with insights into market trends and investment opportunities. This helps users make data-driven decisions.
