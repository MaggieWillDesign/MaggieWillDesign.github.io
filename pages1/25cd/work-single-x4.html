Partir

Partir is a location-based platform for people to easily share and borrow anything within their community with ease.

Project Summary


The Problem

In many situations, individuals may find themselves in need of an item for a limited duration or for one or two specific uses. For instance, when heading to the beach and bringing a cooler, it may not be practical to purchase an item that will only be utilized a handful of times. Young people and those residing in metropolitan areas often have compact living spaces, leaving them with limited storage capacity for various items. Furthermore, an increasing number of individuals are joining the zero waste movement and giving priority to sustainable choices in response to the significant environmental challenge it addresses.

Moreover, there are individuals who possess items they infrequently use and would derive a sense of satisfaction from their purchase while also fostering a stronger connection to their community by sharing these items with others on an as-needed basis. Regrettably, no platform designed for facilitating this type of interaction currently exists.

The Solution

Welcome to the sharing revolution. The Partir network is a sharing hub where users effortlessly connect with others to share, or borrow anything, all while promoting a sense of community and responsible consumption.

Partir is not just about convenience; it's about saving money, reducing waste, and enhancing social bonds. Sharing is the key to a brighter, more sustainable future, and Partir makes it accessible to everyone.

Role: Research, Brand Identity, Brand Voice & Tone, UX, Prototyping, UI, Visual Design, Market Strategy & Design

Time Frame: 5 Week Project, 2023

Value Proposition

Partir provides users with a platform to effortlessly connect with other users to share and borrow anything. Partir saves users money, space, fosters connections, and promotes a sense of community and responsible consumption.

Creating Connections

Through the platform, people have the opportunity to connect with others nearby, fostering a sense of community that might otherwise remain untapped.

Empowering Sustainable Action

The Partir mission drives every aspect of the platform and fuels a commitment to building a sustainable sharing economy. Takeaways
& Next Steps

I found the project gratifying because it addressed an issue that is relatable to my friends and me. I enjoyed bringing the design to life, while learning how to design an app that is helpful to its users. The UI and onboarding was my favorite when working on this project. I found this project particularly rewarding because it addressed a problem that resonated with both me and my friends. Creating the design and transforming it into a functional app was an enjoyable learning experience, aiming to provide value to its users.

Looking back, there are areas that could benefit from improvement, particularly in enhancing the karma points features to gamify the overall user experience. Additionally, there is a need to explore additional avenues for connecting users who share common interests. For instance, individuals passionate about outdoor water activities should have an easier time finding and sharing items amongst themselves, fostering a sense of community. In conclusion, this project allowed me to delve deeper into the realm of design, expanding my proficiency in UX research and UI design.
