Momntm

Momntm is a fitness app featuring customized workouts and unwavering encouragement.

Project Summary


The Problem

Workout apps available in the market often fall short in providing users with personalized fitness options. Many of these apps follow a one-size-fits-all approach, neglecting the diverse range of fitness levels among their user base. As a result, these apps tend to become stagnant, leading to a decline in user engagement and interest due to the lack of customization.

To address this issue, it's essential to offer users a starting point that aligns with their individual fitness levels. This not only allows them to track their progress and witness their growth but also provides the necessary motivation to sustain their fitness journey.

The Solution

The Momntm mission is to empower users to achieve their fitness aspirations. They believe that a healthier and stronger user begins with consistent motivation and gradual progress. The app features customized workouts and the motivation users need to unleash their momentum and conquer challenges. Workouts end, training doesn't

Momntm understands that every fitness journey is unique. The app allows users to tailor workouts to their goals, ensuring steady strides toward a healthier lifestyle. The app fosters a supportive community of like-minded users who share stories, achievements, and encouragement, creating a supportive network to help keep users motivated.

Role: Art Direction, Research, Brand Identity, Brand Voice & Tone, UX, Prototyping, UI, Visual Design, Growth Marketing Strategy & Design

Time Frame: 4 Week Project, 2023

Value Proposition

The Momntm app offers custom fitness plans to users of all levels, along with motivational support from a thriving community to cheer them on. Users enjoy the convenience of tailored workouts, accessible anytime and anywhere, helping them reclaim their time, improve their health, and enhance their well-being

Customized Progress

The app allows users to custom-tailor their workouts to align with their individual goals, ensuring a personally crafted path to a healthier lifestyle.

Daily Encouragement

The Momntm app is dedicated to empowering users with the motivation and confidence to embrace their fitness journey, regardless of their current position on the route to better health.

Takeaways
& Next Steps

With this project, I have gained valuable insights into the diverse approaches users have towards fitness and the recurring challenges they often face. While the app aims to cater to individuals of all fitness levels, there is significant room for further improvement and accessibility solutions. The initial goal was to initiate the process of providing more customization features, moving away from a one-size-fits-all approach.

Future Improvements and Next Steps:

  • Enhanced Accessibility: Focus on expanding accessibility features and brand voice, ensuring the app is usable and beneficial to individuals of varying abilities and needs.

  • Nutrition Coaching: To provide a holistic fitness experience, design a seamless flow for nutrition coaching, helping users make informed dietary choices that complement their workout routines.

  • Community Building: Enhance the social aspects, fostering a sense of community where users can connect, share their experiences, and motivate each other on their fitness journeys. Building a supportive environment is integral to the vision.
