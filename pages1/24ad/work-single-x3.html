First Trade

First Trade is an online trading platform pivoting to excite a new generation of investors by providing an innovative and intuitive product.

Project Summary


The Problem

First Trade is in need of a new identity, voice and tone to reposition itself in the market. With many younger generations being drawn to crpyto platforms due to the innovation and fresh brand identity many traditional platforms are losing market share.

The Solution

First Trade seeks to address this by refreshing its look and providing a platform to intrigue new investors with ai solutions and educational resources.

Role: Research, Brand and UX Audit, Brand Identity, Brand Voice & Tone, UX, Prototyping, UI, Visual Design, Growth Marketing Strategy & Design

Time Frame: 3 Week Project, 2023

Value Proposition

The First Trade platform is a gateway to the revitalized investment experience. The new and improved dashboard offers cutting-edge AI solutions, and educational resources to empower tomorrow's investors today.

Takeaways
& Next Steps

Robin Hood's product has always resonated with me, and it consistently elicits excitement and inspiration when I interact with their app. As a solo designer, my aspiration has been to infuse the same innovative energy into the First Trade brand. I acknowledge that there is still room for significant improvements and a desire to further enrich the identity. With the help of user testing and feedback, the product will evolve in tandem with these considerations:

  • AI Features: The burgeoning AI trend is here to stay, and financial platforms possess immense potential to empower new users and facilitate their journey into investment. I recognize the need for more AI-focused brand messaging and product enhancements to bridge the gap for these novice investors. By integrating AI capabilities, the product will provide users with valuable insights, personalized recommendations, and enhanced user experiences

  • Evolving Identity: The user base is continually expanding and evolving. As such, it's imperative the brand's look and feel adapt to meet the changing expectations and demographics. I am committed to exploring how this evolution can be seamlessly integrated without alienating existing users. Through thoughtful design and user-centric development, I want to ensure that the evolving identity remains inviting and inclusive, fostering a sense of belonging for both new and loyal users alike.
